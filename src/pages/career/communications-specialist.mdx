## Communications Intern

Date of Posting: 13 September 2022

## About the Position
Tattle is looking for a communications intern to work on communications and community building for (Uli)[uli.tattle.co.in].          

### Tasks: 
* Phone and email communication with potential individual and organisational users of the plug-in
* Follow-up communication with users
* Online event organising communication and organising support as part of community building process
* Collating feedback and testimonial from users 
* Collation of data and support on report writing

### Required skills: 
* Should have working knowledge of using Twitter as a platform
* Should be interested in marketing
* Should have talking skills and ease in starting conversations with strangers in both English and Hindi
* Would be required to initiate phone conversations with potential users of our plug-in so the person should be comfortable with people interactions
* Should interested and keen on working on gender and other intersectional issues
* Should ready to learn and engage with new technology ideas and concepts
* Individuals with prior experience with working on social media, especially Twitter 
* Individuals with prior understanding and interest in technology will be preferred
* Since this is a project related to gender, individuals from gender and sexual minorities (women, LGBTIQA+) and other marginalised identities will be given preference. 

**Start Date:** ASAP.
**Location:** Remote
**Duration:** Full-Time/ Part-time
**Remuneration:** Based on time availability and scope of work. 
**Deadline:** Rolling 

## About Tattle
Tattle is a civic tech project committed to claiming a bigger space for civic action against misinformation and hate speech in India. We're a cross-disciplinary team of engineers and researchers. We value inter-disciplinary thinking and unconventional life experiences. We provide space to incorporate unique individual learnings in all that we make. We provide a stress free work environment with a flexible 8 hours workday policy. 


We are a fully remote team that communicates asynchronously.

### We Value Diversity and Multiplicity of Experiences
At Tattle, we cherish diversity of experiences and perspectives. It adds value to our work. To this end, we strongly encourage candidates, even if they feel they don't meet all the above listed criteria but find alignment with the project and are driven to learn, to apply.

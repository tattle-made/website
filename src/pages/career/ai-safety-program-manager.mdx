## About the Position
Tattle is a civic tech organization that build tools and datasets to enable civic action against online harms. We support researchers as well as conduct in-house research on platform governance, gendered harassment, AI Safety and 'responsible' AI.

We are looking for a meticulous project manager to coordinate across a number of tracks in our growing AI Safety track. The projects span developing research frameworks, consulting organizations on their AI safety practices and building tooling for safe and resposible AI. You can see some of our work here:
* [https://tattle.co.in/](https://tattle.co.in/)

## Consultant, Data Governance: Uli

Date of Posting: 15 February 2023

## About the Position
[Uli](uli.tattle.co.in) is a project to helps people reclaim agency in protecting themselves from online gender based violence. Uli is a response to:
- The disproportionate harassment people of marginalized genders receive in India
- The inadequate attention given by platforms to non-anglophone social media users.  
The tool was piloted in August 2022, for three languages and for Twitter. It was built in partnership with various [researchers and gender rights organizations in India and South Asia](https://uli.tattle.co.in/about). The focus for this year is to grow the partnerships and expand the corpus of data and resources driving Uli, while also working on a revenue model. 

We're looking for a research consultant to study data governance models of social good projects to conceptualize an ethical data governance model for Uli. This entails looking at the entire ML development flow, from recruiting data donors to practices for immediate and long-term compensation. The consultant will be expected to share their findings and get frequent feedback from the team and partner organizations. The end deliverable is an external facing report.
